Her Story

Dr. Samar Yehia is a leading aesthetic medicine physician renowned for her signature Diamond Lips technique — a proprietary approach that ensures every lip augmentation is harmonized with the patient's unique smile architecture.

With over eight years of clinical experience and training from leading international institutions, Dr. Samar combines deep anatomical knowledge with an artist's eye to deliver results that look natural, feel beautiful, and last.

Her philosophy is simple: enhance, never overpower. Each treatment is a collaboration between doctor and patient — a conversation about confidence, identity, and the version of yourself you've always envisioned.
